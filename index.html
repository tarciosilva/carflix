FERRARI
PURO SANGUE

The Ferrari Purosangue is the Maranello brand's first SUV. With 725 horsepower, an aspirated V12 engine and 4x4 architecture, it combines luxury and sportiness. Its lines are undeniably Ferrari, and the vehicle is almost five meters long and almost 1.6 meters high. Furthermore, it is the brand's first model with space for four people.
